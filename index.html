Eureka! 2026 Update

We are sorry!

Due to widespread participant unavailability on 8–9 April, along with the approaching end-semester examinations and lab schedules, we regret to announce that Eureka! will not be held as planned this year. Unfortunately, rescheduling is not feasible under the current constraints.

We sincerely apologize for any inconvenience caused and deeply appreciate your enthusiasm and support.

All registration fees (passes and competitions) will be refunded by the end of next week. Please refer to Refund Policy on this website.

Thank you for your understanding.

About Eureka! 2026

Eureka! 2026 is the inaugural science fest of the School of Basic Sciences, CSJMU (accredited NAAC A++), Kanpur, envisioned as a large-scale academic and cultural platform bringing together science, innovation, public engagement, e-gaming, and cultural celebration.

Once in Lifetime Experience

  • Academic events and scientific activities.
  • Innovation and youth engagement
  • E-gaming attractions.
  • Pronite performances (Sufi Night and Bollywood Eve) and entertainment segments.

Three Competition Arenas

Eureka! brings together three distinct streams of participation, each with its own energy, creativity, and challenge.

Academic Competitions

Quizzes, model contests, debates, Project Pokémon, Hogwarts Hunt and intellectually engaging challenges.

e-Sports Competitions

E-gaming with team in BGMI and Free-Fire contests built around skill, focus, teamwork, and competitive spirit.

Cultural Competitions

Performance-based events celebrating expression, rhythm, stage presence, and creativity.
